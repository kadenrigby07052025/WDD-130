Overview

Purpose

To provide adventure seekers with engaging, easy-to-access information about white water rafting trips, safety guidelines, booking options, and local rafting conditions to grow awareness and bookings for White Rapids Rafting.

Audience

Our target audience is adults aged 18 to 45 who enjoy outdoor adventure sports, travel, and nature. They value safety, excitement, and convenience, and typically access our site via smartphones while planning trips, or desktops while researching rafting packages.
