Our Rivers

Main Fork of the Salmon River — Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return winds the Salmon River. It was given the nickname “The River of No Return” because visitors often fell in love with its rugged beauty and never wanted to leave.

Elk and deer graze on the hillsides, while bear and bighorn sheep are also commonly sighted. Towering ponderosa pines line the corridor, shading sandy beaches and creating amazing campsites.
