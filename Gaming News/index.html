Recent Releases in One Glance

These are quick hit thoughts on games I have been playing recently. If you just want to know if something is worth your time before a full review drops, start here.

Starfield

Huge sandbox and cool ship building, but the story pacing is uneven and the worlds feel a little too empty.

Helldivers 2

Absolute chaos with friends and some of the best co op missions I have played in years.

Dead Space Remake

Faithful to the original while tightening combat and atmosphere, a must play for horror fans.

Doom: The Dark Ages

The story is more in the foreground compared to previous entries. Combat is fun and satisfying.

Civilization 7

A graphical improvement over the previous entry. Perfect starting point for the casual gamer.

Halo Infinite

Rough launch but in a much better place now. A good time!
